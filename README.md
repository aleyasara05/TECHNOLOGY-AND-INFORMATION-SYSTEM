# Technology and Information System

## Course Synopsis
The course outlines introduction to concepts of technology and information systems and discusses how such concepts benefit the individual, organization, and society. Information system types, hardware, software, networking, management of data, and other issues relating to security and ethics of digital transformation shall also be covered. Through lectures and class discussion and with hands-on activities and projects designed to improve problem-solving and teamwork by applying critical thinking, students shall be able to analyze problems associated with information systems and implement simple solutions for real-world applications.
